About Me

I am a third year graduate student at the University of Nebraska, Lincoln, advised by Eloísa Grifo. Broadly speaking, I am primarily interested in commutative algebra and its intersection with homological algebra.

I am originally from the Los Angeles area, and lived there for most of my life until coming to UNL. More specifically, I grew up in the Pasadena area and did my undergraduate studies at UCLA. Afterwards I completed two years of a PhD program at UC Riverside before transferring to Nebraska to continue my studies. I have a wide range of interests outside of math but some of the things I've enjoyed as of late are running, playing soccer, exploring the outdoors, and trying new foods and drinks.
